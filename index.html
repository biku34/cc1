Why CollegeCupid ?

In today’s fast-paced, digital world, meeting new people often feels transactional. CollegeCupid is about slowing down and sharing meaningful conversations. It’s about connecting without expectations—just two people, a quick badminton match, and the chance to learn something new about each other.

How it works ?

Register with Us

Sign up and share a few details about yourself, including your interests, hobbies, and what you're looking for in a connection.

We Match You

Using your preferences, we find people with similar interests who share your enthusiasm for badminton and meaningful conversations.

Get Notified

Once a match is found, we’ll notify you to schedule a friendly badminton game over the weekend.

Play, Connect, and Bond

Badminton isn’t just about the game; it’s a fun and energetic way to break the ice, discover shared interests, and spark deeper conversations. As you play, you’ll not only enjoy the thrill of the match but also create opportunities for more meaningful connections.
